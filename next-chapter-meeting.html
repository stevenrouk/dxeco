Our next Chapter Meeting is TBD.

Chapter Meetings are where we deliberate over proposed amendments to the Bylaws and other items to be voted on. Although there can't be changes made to proposed items (these must be decided on and put forward for the following Chapter Meeting), the Chapter Meeting is your place to express your thoughts about any given item and suggest whether you think it should be approved or not!

Items to Be Voted On

Item #1—Amendment to the Bylaws—Social Media Admins

The following text will be inserted to the Bylaws as Article 5 Section 1.17, pushing back the following Section items by one bullet point:

The social media accounts of the page that is currently known as “Direct Action Everywhere - Colorado” (https://www.facebook.com/dxecolorado/) and the Facebook Group known as “DxE Colorado Community - Animal Rights Denver, Boulder, Fort Collins” (https://www.facebook.com/groups/DxEColorado/) will be administered by the members that populate the Coordinating Committee. In addition, members of Official Working Groups who post events should be added as editors. The Coordinating Committee can grant editing access to others as they see fit.

Item #2—Amendment to the Bylaws—Replace "Unofficial Groups" with "Grassroots Philosophy"

Change the name of Article 4 Section 1 from "Unofficial Groups" to "Grassroots Philosophy".

Item #3—Amendment to the Bylaws—Replace one week with two weeks

In Article 6.2.c, change "one week" to "two weeks".
