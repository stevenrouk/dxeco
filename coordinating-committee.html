March Meeting Agendas

Although there are many things discussed at each Coordinating Committee meeting, these are the overall topics guiding discussion at each meeting during the month.

For the specific agenda items that we will cover at each meeting, view the notes document (folder linked below) for the relevant meeting date. Agenda items will be placed in the document before the meeting.

3/6:

  1. Amendments to the Bylaws: what ideas were brought up at the March Chapter Meeting?
  2. Official Working Groups: which groups need to be formed, or are likely to be formed?

3/13:

  1. Chapter vision, goals, strategy, and tactics
  2. Official Working Group formation: reviewing and discussing proposals

3/20:

  1. Calendar: do we have an event calendar solution, and how is it working?
  2. Conflict management, DxE Colorado culture, and group dynamics

3/27:

  1. CALF: organizing the efforts of grassroots groups along the Front Range
  2. ICAS Conference: are we, or how are we, collaborating with the ICAS Conference?
